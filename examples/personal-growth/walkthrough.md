# Personal Growth — Mirror Palace Walkthrough

You're using Mirror Palace primarily for self-understanding and personal development. Career, relationships, health, identity — you want a system that helps you see clearly and act intentionally.

## Recommended Frameworks (Start Here)

1. **Awareness as Intervention** — The foundation. Start by observing patterns without trying to fix them. Naming reduces their power.
2. **Failure Modes** — Identify your top 3 recurring failure modes. Which patterns show up under stress?
3. **Four-F Survival Types** — Understand your stress response. Fight, flight, freeze, or fawn? This shapes everything downstream.
4. **Ikigai Diagnostic** — Use it as a gap-locator. Where's the misalignment between love, skill, need, and earning?
5. **Structured Self-Coaching** — Commit to a weekly 30-minute self-coaching session. This is the practice that makes everything else work.

## Status Domains — All 10

For personal growth, initialize ALL domains. Even if some are sparse at first — the gaps are informative:

- **Money & Finances** — Financial stress is real, not philosophical
- **Career & Work** — What you do and whether it aligns
- **Health & Fitness** — Foundation for everything else
- **Fun & Recreation** — Neglecting this is a pattern worth catching
- **Environment** — Does your physical space support your goals?
- **Community** — Belonging matters more than most people admit
- **Family & Friends** — Inherited patterns live here
- **Partner & Love** — Where attachment and growth intersect
- **Personal Growth** — What you're actively learning and practicing
- **Spirituality** — Whatever this means to you — depth, meaning, practice

## Personality Assessments

Complete all three templates:
1. **Big Five** — Understand your trait profile. High openness + low conscientiousness has different implications than high conscientiousness + low openness.
2. **Enneagram** — Understand your core motivation and stress/growth paths.
3. **MBTI** — Understand your cognitive function stack and inferior function stress patterns.

The scan skill can partially populate these from your writing and conversation patterns.

## People Records — Key Relationships

For your 5-10 most important relationships:
- Track support% and challenge% — the best relationships are high in both
- List needs met and unmet — this reveals where you're settling and where you're thriving
- Note communication quality — are you being real or editing?

## Decisions Ledger

Your current open life decisions. Even listing them is clarifying:
- Career direction
- Relationship status
- Where to live
- How to spend time
- What to invest in (money, energy, attention)

Apply reversibility-classification to each. You'll likely find most are more reversible than they feel.

4. **Weekly**: Review The Mirror's reflection if deployed
