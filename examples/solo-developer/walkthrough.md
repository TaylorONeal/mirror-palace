# Solo Developer — Mirror Palace Walkthrough

You're a solo developer or indie maker. You build things alone, juggle multiple projects, and struggle with prioritization, shipping, and the isolation of solo work.

## Recommended Frameworks (Start Here)

1. **Reversibility Classification** — Most of your decisions are two-way doors. Stop agonizing over tool choices, project names, and architecture decisions. Ship and iterate.
2. **ADHD Design Rules** — Even without ADHD, solo work demands executive function support. Hide your backlog. One thing at a time.
3. **North Star Test** — For every feature: does the user know what to do, feel confident, do it, feel good? If not, cut it.
4. **Failure Modes** — Your top risks: Project Sprawl (too many open projects), Freeze/Overthinking (perfectionism), Romanticizing Other Selves (fantasy projects vs shipping the current one).
5. **Systems Over Goals** — Build a daily shipping practice instead of chasing "launch day."

## Recommended Agent Archetypes

| Archetype | Why |
|-----------|-----|
| The Briefer | Daily "what matters today" synthesis. Prevents drift. |
| The Tracker | Compares your stated priorities to your actual work. Catches sprawl early. |

## Status Domains to Initialize

| Domain | Why |
|--------|-----|
| Career & Work | Track projects, stages, leverage scores |
| Money & Finances | Runway awareness — how long can you sustain solo work? |
| Health & Fitness | Solo work erodes health routines. Track it. |
| Fun & Recreation | Burnout prevention. When did you last do something non-productive for fun? |

## Decisions Ledger Starters

- Should I keep working on Project X or pivot to Y?
- Should I charge for this or keep it free?
- Should I take a contract gig or stay focused on my product?

Apply reversibility-classification to each. Most are two-way doors.

## Daily Briefing Customization

Adjust the morning briefing to focus on:
- What's the ONE thing to ship today?
- Is the current project in sprawl? (Check: how many open threads?)
- Energy check → match to the right type of work
