# Childhood Emotional Neglect

A framework for understanding the impact of what was missing in childhood -- not what happened, but what did not happen. Maps how the absence of emotional attunement, validation, and responsiveness produces chronic emptiness, difficulty identifying feelings and needs, counter-dependence, and the "fatal flaw" belief. Recovery involves learning to feel, not just think.

## Quick Reference
- **Best for**: Understanding chronic emptiness, difficulty with feelings/needs, and the "good childhood" paradox
- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Time to apply**: Initial assessment 30-45 minutes; recovery is long-term
- **Pairs with**: Four-F Survival Types, Emotionally Immature Parents, Embodied Awareness
