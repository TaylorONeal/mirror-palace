# EFT Relationship Lens

A relationship-specific framework for examining attachment alarm, protest behavior, withdrawal, and rupture/repair dynamics. Best used as an overlay for close-bond analysis, not as a total theory of life.

## Quick Reference
- **Best for**: understanding repeated romantic conflict beneath the surface topic
- **Complexity**: medium
- **Time to apply**: 10-20 minutes
- **Pairs with**: Needs & Feelings Clarity, Psychological Contracts, Family Systems Differentiation
