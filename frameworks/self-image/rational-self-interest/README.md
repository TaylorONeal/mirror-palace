# Rational Self-Interest

A framework for living in alignment with your own values, through your own reasoning, in a way that supports your flourishing and harms no one. Draws on the philosophical tradition of rational self-interest -- the idea that your life is your central responsibility, that happiness is achievable through principled action, and that the universe is open to your achievement. This is not about taking from others. It is about building a life you can be genuinely proud of.

## Quick Reference
- **Best for**: Clarifying personal values, resolving guilt around self-prioritization, building a purposeful life through reason
- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Time to apply**: Initial values audit takes 1-2 hours; ongoing practice through daily decisions
- **Pairs with**: Systems Over Goals, Identity Reinforcement, North Star Test, Ikigai Diagnostic
