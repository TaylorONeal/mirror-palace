# Regret Minimization

Project yourself to age 80 and ask: "Will I regret NOT doing this?" Minimize lifetime regret, not short-term discomfort. Over a lifetime, regret of inaction almost always exceeds regret of action.

## Quick Reference
- **Best for**: Meaningful life decisions blocked by fear, not logic
- **Complexity**: Low
- **Time to apply**: 5-10 minutes
- **Pairs with**: Reversibility Classification, Ikigai Diagnostic, Identity Reinforcement
