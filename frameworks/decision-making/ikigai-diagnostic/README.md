# Ikigai Diagnostic

A 4-circle diagnostic (not prescription) used as a gap-locator, not a destination-drawer. Each circle has a characteristic trap: love changes more than you think, skill is where people lie to themselves, need is least falsifiable, and earning is most legible but both strength and trap.

## Quick Reference
- **Best for**: Career direction, life purpose assessment, identifying the binding constraint on fulfillment
- **Complexity**: High
- **Time to apply**: 30-60 minutes for full assessment; revisit quarterly
- **Pairs with**: Regret Minimization, Concept Formation, Identity Reinforcement, Reversibility Classification
