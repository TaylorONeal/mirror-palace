# Structured Self-Coaching

A weekly 30-minute written protocol that converts emotional experience into behavioral commitment through five sequential questions: What happened? What did I feel? What pattern is this? What will I do differently? What is the felt truth? The power is in the structure, the writing, and the consistency over time.

## Quick Reference
- **Best for**: Converting insight into action through regular structured reflection
- **Complexity**: Low (simple protocol, discipline is the challenge)
- **Time to apply**: 30 minutes weekly
- **Pairs with**: Stories vs. Facts, Failure Modes, Embodied Awareness

- **template.md** -- Weekly worksheet with all five questions, commitment review, and session tracking
