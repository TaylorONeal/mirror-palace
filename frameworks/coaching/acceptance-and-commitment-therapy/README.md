# Acceptance & Commitment Therapy

A framework for reducing fusion with thoughts, allowing experience without collapse, clarifying values, and moving into committed action. Best used when someone is stuck in internal struggle and needs a cleaner relationship to thought, feeling, and next action.

## Quick Reference
- **Best for**: shame spirals, comparison, transition stress, avoidance loops, and decisions distorted by fusion or discomfort-avoidance
- **Complexity**: medium
- **Time to apply**: 10-20 minutes
- **Pairs with**: Needs & Feelings Clarity, Stories vs Facts, Awareness as Intervention, Embodied Awareness
