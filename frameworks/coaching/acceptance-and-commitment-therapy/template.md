## 5. Needs and Feelings Check
Before moving too fast into analysis, separate signal from accusation.

- What happened factually?
- What did I actually feel?
- What need or value seems touched?
- Am I naming a need, or naming a preferred strategy?
- Are any faux feelings present (ignored, rejected, abandoned, misunderstood, dismissed, attacked, trapped, invalidated)? If yes, translate them.

## 8. Guardrails
Check for misuse before concluding.

- [ ] I am not using acceptance to stay in obvious harm.
- [ ] I am not using the framework as overanalysis.
- [ ] I am not waiting to feel perfect before acting.
- [ ] The next action is concrete, not symbolic.
