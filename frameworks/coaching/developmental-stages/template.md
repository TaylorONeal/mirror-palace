## Part 1: Domain-Level Stage Assessment

For each life domain, assess which stage you are currently in.

**Safety indicators**: Difficulty being honest, hypervigilance, performing rather than being authentic, emotional volatility or shutdown.

**Structure indicators**: Can be honest but needs clear expectations, benefits from accountability, developing routines but not yet self-sustaining.

**Autonomy indicators**: Self-directing, self-correcting, comfortable with ambiguity, choosing constraints rather than needing them imposed.
