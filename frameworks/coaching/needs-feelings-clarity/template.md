# Needs & Feelings Clarity Template

## Situation
What happened, factually?

## Surface reaction
What did I first say, think, or want to do?

## Clean feeling translation
If I remove accusation-language, what am I actually feeling?
- sad
- angry
- anxious
- lonely
- ashamed
- hurt
- relieved
- glad
- other:

## Faux feeling check
Did I use any of these?
- ignored
- abandoned
- misunderstood
- dismissed
- attacked
- trapped
- invalidated
- rejected

If yes, translate them:
- What happened?
- What did I actually feel?

## Need / value cluster
Which need clusters seem touched?
- physical regulation
- safety / steadiness
- connection / belonging
- autonomy / agency
- recognition / mattering
- meaning / direction
- growth / competence
- play / aliveness
- integrity / alignment

## Strategy check
What am I calling a need that is actually a tactic?
Examples:
- texting back
- apologizing
- choosing me
- reassuring me
- fixing it immediately

## Best next move
What action would serve the deeper need most cleanly?

## Watchout
Am I using this framework to clarify, or to avoid a direct decision?
