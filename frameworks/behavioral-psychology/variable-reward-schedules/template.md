## Reward Quality Check

- [ ] Specific (references actual behavior, not generic)
- [ ] Contextual (relevant to current state)
- [ ] Varied in form (not always the same message)
- [ ] Appropriately scaled (small rewards for small actions)
- [ ] Non-reward occasions preserved (not rewarding everything)

## Anti-Pattern Check

- [ ] NOT all variable, no deterministic (every action is acknowledged)
- [ ] NOT variable punishment (negative outcomes are never randomized)
- [ ] NOT too-low probability (above 20% for routine completions)
- [ ] NOT content-predictable (reward messages vary, not just timing)
- [ ] NOT dependency-creating (serves user-chosen behaviors, not platform goals)
