# Subconscious Repatterning

A framework explaining why cognition alone does not change deep patterns. Maps three layers -- conscious thought, emotional response, and nervous system/body -- and provides bridge practices for connecting cognitive understanding to somatic change. The core insight: the body stores patterns the mind has "resolved," and body-level change requires body-level work.

## Quick Reference
- **Best for**: Understanding why insight does not equal change and what to do about it
- **Complexity**: High (simple concept, demanding practice)
- **Time to apply**: Initial mapping 30 minutes; practice is ongoing over months
- **Pairs with**: Embodied Awareness, Four-F Survival Types, Five Wounds
