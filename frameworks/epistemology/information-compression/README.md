# Information Compression

A priority hierarchy for deciding what to preserve and what to discard as context accumulates. Constraints > Goals > Commitments > Definitions > Critical Facts > Open Questions. Discard redundant phrasing, emotional filler, resolved subthreads, and stylistic noise.
