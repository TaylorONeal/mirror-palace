# Enneagram

A motivation-based personality system describing nine types, each organized around a core fear and core desire. Includes wings (adjacent type influence), integration/disintegration lines (growth and stress movements), three centers of intelligence (body, heart, head), and relational dynamics. The Enneagram maps why you do what you do, not just what you do.

## Quick Reference
- **Best for**: Understanding motivational roots, stress deterioration patterns, and growth direction
- **Complexity**: High (nine types with wings, arrows, and centers)
- **Time to apply**: 30-45 minutes for type identification; ongoing integration
- **Pairs with**: Big Five (trait detail), MBTI (cognitive functions), Five Wounds (overlapping patterns)
