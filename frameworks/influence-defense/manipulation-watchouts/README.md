# Manipulation Watchouts

DEFENSIVELY FRAMED. Six influence techniques to recognize when they are used on you: reciprocity traps, commitment/consistency exploitation, social proof pressure, authority inflation, scarcity manufacturing, and liking manipulation. Each with what it looks like, why it works psychologically, how to spot it, and how to respond without escalating. The core defense against all six: slow down and take time. Manipulation depends on immediacy.

## Quick Reference
- **Best for**: Recognizing influence that has crossed into manipulation; protecting decision autonomy
- **Complexity**: Medium (6 techniques, each with recognition pattern and response strategy)
- **Time to apply**: 10-15 minutes for situation analysis; ongoing recognition skill
- **Pairs with**: Embodied Awareness (gut feelings detect manipulation first), Psychological Contracts (manipulation creates invisible contracts), Failure Modes (personal vulnerabilities to specific techniques)

## Files
- **theory.md** -- All 6 techniques: what they look like, why they work, how to spot, how to respond. Meta-skill of separating influence from manipulation.
- **template.md** -- Situation analysis worksheet, technique checklist, personal vulnerability map, response scripts
- **agent-prompt.md** -- Protocol for helping users recognize and respond to manipulation without creating paranoia
