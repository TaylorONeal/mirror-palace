## Part 4: Response Scripts

Pre-written responses for situations where you need time. Adapt to your voice.

**General delay**: "Let me think about that and get back to you."

**Reciprocity**: "I appreciate that. I want to consider the request on its own merits."

**Commitment**: "I did say that, and I have since reconsidered. I want to make a fresh decision."

**Social proof**: "I hear that others feel that way. I need to evaluate this for my situation."

**Authority**: "I respect your expertise. Can you help me understand the reasoning?"

**Scarcity**: "I need [specific time] to decide. If it does not survive that, it was not right for me."

**Liking**: "I enjoy our connection. I still want to evaluate this separately."
