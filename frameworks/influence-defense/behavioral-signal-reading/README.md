# Behavioral Signal Reading

A defensive framework for reading authority projection, identity commands, micro-expressions, and status plays. Focuses on recognizing when someone is performing authority rather than demonstrating it, when you are being assigned an identity that serves someone else's agenda, when words and behavior are incongruent, and when status moves are positioning you without your awareness.

## Quick Reference
- **Best for**: Understanding why an interaction feels off and reading what is actually being communicated
- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Time to apply**: 15-20 minutes per interaction analysis; ongoing awareness practice
- **Pairs with**: Leverage Point Awareness, Manipulation Watchouts, Stories vs. Facts
