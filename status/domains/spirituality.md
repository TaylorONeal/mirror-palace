## Column Definitions

- **Practice Type**: The form of spiritual engagement — `meditation`, `prayer`, `contemplation`, `breathwork`, `movement` (yoga, tai chi), `study`, `ritual`, `community-worship`, `nature`, `journaling`, `service`
- **Frequency**: How often the practice happens — `daily`, `several-times-weekly`, `weekly`, `sporadic`, `lapsed`
- **Depth Score**: 0-100 rating of experiential depth. High = genuine contact with something beyond surface, altered states, real insight. Low = going through the motions.
- **Integration with Daily Life**: 0-100 rating of how much the practice affects the other 23 hours. High = it changes how you show up everywhere. Low = isolated to the practice time, no carryover.
