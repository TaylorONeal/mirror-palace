## Column Definitions

- **Stage**: Relationship phase — `single`, `dating`, `early-relationship`, `committed`, `cohabiting`, `married`, `separating`, `co-parenting`, `reconnecting`
- **Communication Quality**: 0-100 rating. High = honest, direct, emotionally safe conversations. Low = avoidance, passive-aggression, or constant conflict.
- **Polarity Score**: 0-100 rating of attraction and dynamic energy. High = strong pull, excitement, differentiation maintained. Low = roommate dynamic, merged identities, loss of spark.
- **Growth Trajectory**: Direction of the relationship — `growing` (deepening, expanding), `stable` (maintaining, comfortable), `stagnant` (not growing, not declining), `declining` (deteriorating, distancing), `crisis` (immediate attention needed)
