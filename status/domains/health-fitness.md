## Relevant Frameworks
- `behavior-equation` — Is the health behavior easy enough to actually do?
- `habit-loop-design` — What's the cue-routine-reward for health habits?
- `identity-reinforcement` — "I am someone who exercises" vs "I should exercise"
- `embodied-awareness` — What is the body communicating?
- `subconscious-repatterning` — Are old patterns overriding conscious health intentions?
- `executive-function-model` — Which executive function is the bottleneck for health habits?

## Review Cadence
Weekly for active medical issues or new habit formation. Biweekly for maintenance. Track energy impact trends monthly.
