## Relevant Frameworks
- `north-star-test` — Is recreation aligned with what actually matters, or just filling time?
- `behavior-equation` — Is fun accessible enough to actually happen?
- `distortion-detection` — "I don't deserve to have fun until X is done" (should-statement)
- `systems-over-goals` — Build fun into the system, don't make it a reward

## Review Cadence
Biweekly. Flag if no fun activity has happened in 14+ days. Monthly check on recharge scores and activity balance.
