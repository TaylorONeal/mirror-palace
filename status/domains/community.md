## Column Definitions

- **Group/Scene**: The community, group, or scene (e.g., "local tech meetup", "online writing group", "neighborhood", "church", "gym community")
- **Role**: Your function in the group — `member`, `organizer`, `leader`, `lurker`, `contributor`, `mentor`, `newcomer`
- **Attendance Frequency**: How often you engage — `weekly`, `biweekly`, `monthly`, `sporadic`, `lapsed`
- **Belonging Score**: 0-100 rating of how much you feel you genuinely belong. High = accepted, known, valued. Low = peripheral, performative, or anxious.

## Relevant Frameworks
- `identity-reinforcement` — Communities shape identity. Are they reinforcing who you want to be?
- `psychological-contracts` — What invisible agreements exist with these groups?
- `north-star-test` — Does this community involvement serve what matters most?
- `family-systems-differentiation` — Can you be yourself in this group, or do you adapt to fit?

## Review Cadence
Monthly. Flag if belonging score drops below 40 or attendance goes to "lapsed." Quarterly strategic review of community portfolio.
