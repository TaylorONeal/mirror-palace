## Column Definitions

- **Location**: Where this issue exists — `home`, `work`, `third-place` (cafe, gym, coworking), `digital`, `transit`
- **Issue Type**: Nature of the environmental factor — `physical` (furniture, layout, cleanliness), `digital` (tools, notifications, screen setup), `social` (noise, people, energy), `systemic` (commute, access, cost)
- **Friction Level**: 0-10 scale. How much daily friction does this issue create? 0 = seamless. 10 = constant irritation or obstacle.
