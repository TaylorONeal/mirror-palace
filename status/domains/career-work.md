## Column Definitions

- **Role Type**: The nature of the work (e.g., "employee", "founder", "freelance", "advisor", "side-project")
- **Company/Project**: Where this work is happening. Can be an organization name or project name.
- **Stage**: Current phase — `exploring` (researching, considering), `active` (engaged, doing the work), `committed` (all-in, long-term)
- **Leverage Score**: 0-100 rating of how much this work compounds over time. High leverage = builds skills, reputation, network, or assets that pay off later. Low leverage = trades time for money with no compounding.

## Relevant Frameworks
- `ikigai-diagnostic` — Does this work sit at the intersection of love, skill, need, and pay?
- `north-star-test` — Is this the most important thing to be working on?
- `regret-minimization` — At 80, will you regret not pursuing this?
- `jobs-to-be-done` — What job is this career actually doing for you? (identity? security? purpose?)
- `time-blindness` — Are deadlines and estimates realistic?

## Review Cadence
Weekly when job-searching or in transition. Biweekly when stable. Monthly strategic review of leverage scores and pipeline.
